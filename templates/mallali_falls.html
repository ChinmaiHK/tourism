Mallalli Falls

Mallalli Falls is one of the most spectacular waterfalls in Coorg, formed by the Kumaradhara River as it plunges from a height of 200 feet. Surrounded by lush green forests and misty hills, the waterfall is a breathtaking sight, especially during the monsoon season.

Reaching the falls requires a short trek through picturesque trails, making it a favorite spot for adventure enthusiasts. The soothing sound of cascading water and the cool, refreshing atmosphere create a perfect setting for relaxation and photography.

Located near Somwarpet, about 26 km from Madikeri, Mallalli Falls is an offbeat yet rewarding destination. It is best visited between June and September when the waterfall is at its peak flow.
