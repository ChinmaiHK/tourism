Raja’s Seat

Raja’s Seat is a popular viewpoint in Coorg, known for its breathtaking sunset and panoramic views of the lush valleys and rolling hills. Historically, it was the favorite recreational spot of the Kodagu kings, who would come here to relax and admire the scenic beauty.

The well-maintained garden surrounding Raja’s Seat is adorned with seasonal flowers and a musical fountain, enhancing the tranquil experience. The viewpoint is best visited early in the morning or during sunset when the golden hues paint the sky beautifully.

Located in Madikeri town, Raja’s Seat is easily accessible by road. The spot is ideal for visitors looking to unwind in nature while capturing stunning photographs of the Coorg landscape.
