Dubare Elephant Camp

Dubare Elephant Camp is a must-visit destination for wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers. Situated on the banks of the Cauvery River, this camp offers visitors a unique opportunity to interact with elephants, observe their daily routine, and even participate in activities like feeding and bathing them.

Apart from elephants, the camp is home to various bird species and wildlife, making it a paradise for nature photographers. Visitors can also indulge in activities like river rafting, coracle rides, and nature walks along the serene riverbanks.

Dubare Elephant Camp is located about 15 km from Kushalnagar and can be reached via a short boat ride across the river. It is an educational and immersive experience, ideal for families, wildlife lovers, and adventure seekers.
