Explore Coorg - The Scotland of India

Abbey Falls

Abbey Falls is a breathtaking waterfall surrounded by coffee plantations and lush greenery.

Raja’s Seat

Raja’s Seat offers a mesmerizing sunset view and was a favorite retreat for Kodagu kings.

Dubare Elephant Camp

Dubare Elephant Camp provides an opportunity to interact with elephants and learn about their conservation.

Nisargadhama

Nisargadhama is a beautiful island on the River Kaveri, known for its bamboo groves and deer park.

Talakaveri

Talakaveri is the birthplace of the Kaveri River and a popular pilgrimage site.

Mallali Falls

Mallali Falls is a stunning waterfall located in the foothills of the Pushpagiri Mountains.

Mandalpatti

Mandalpatti is a magnificent and unique site in Coorg, Karnataka , overlooking the grasslands of Pushpagiri forests in the Western Ghats.
