Nisargadhama

Nisargadhama is a spectacular waterfall located amidst coffee plantations in Coorg. The falls cascade down from a height of 70 feet, creating a mesmerizing sight, especially during the monsoon season. A hanging bridge near the falls provides a stunning view, making it a popular spot for tourists and nature lovers.

Visitors can enjoy the sound of gushing water, the cool misty breeze, and the lush greenery surrounding the falls. The trek to Nisargadhama is short but scenic, passing through thick coffee and spice estates. It is a great spot for photography, relaxation, and soaking in the natural beauty of Coorg. The best time to visit is from July to October, when the falls are at their fullest.

Nisargadhama is located about 8 km from Madikeri town, making it easily accessible by road. Parking is available near the entrance, and a short walk through the plantations leads to the viewpoint. Whether you're a nature enthusiast, a photographer, or just looking for a peaceful retreat, Nisargadhama is a must-visit destination in Coorg.
